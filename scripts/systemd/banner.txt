*** EWM Apple 1 *** one fresh machine per connection ***

You are at the Woz Monitor ('\' prompt). Try:

  FF00.FF1F      dump a piece of the monitor ROM
  E000R          start Integer BASIC ('>' prompt)

Meta-R (or telnet's 'send brk') is the RESET button.
Ctrl-] then 'quit' leaves telnet. Have fun!
